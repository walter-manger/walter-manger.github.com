Learn Other Things

Sometimes I find myself so hung up on the things that I am trying to accomplish at work that it leaks over into the time that I’ve set aside to learn other things. I am a strong believer in the subconsious as a tool for solving complex problems. If you are laser focused on something for a length of time and it absorbs your thoughts completely even when you’re not working on it, there is no time for your subconsious to work on it.

There has been a lot of research about the advantages of moving away from a problem.
